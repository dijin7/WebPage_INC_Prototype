What is the origin of this Soy?

Soy is a native plant from Asia, cultivated mainly in China for thousands of years, Being used as a food with high nutritional value. With time its production has increased globally, with its high versatility and high protein.

Our soybean varietes have different organic genetic markers and top of the line selection from all over the world, which as a result gives soybeans adapted to many enviroment, resistant to different diseases and with a very high protein suitable for a nearly infinite uses.

What's the procces of the soy?

We have Soy with very early maturity, which means that the process of planting and harvesting is quick. The beans germinate fast in very little time, and in a few weeks, they generate strong stems and leaves with a large quantity of branches, so that in no time, little white or purple flowers that are provided along with all the information about the soy.
